## Why We're Starting This Blog

At CampaignAgent, we've spent years working with CMOs and marketing operations leaders who face the same challenges: fragmented campaign data, unclear attribution, and too much time spent on manual reporting instead of strategic work.

This blog is our way of sharing what we've learned—practical guidance you can use whether or not you're a CampaignAgent customer.

## What You Can Expect

We'll cover topics like:

- **Campaign Governance**: How to structure approvals, budgets, and workflows so nothing falls through the cracks
- **Attribution Best Practices**: Moving beyond last-touch to understand what's actually driving revenue
- **AI in Marketing Ops**: Where AI adds real value (and where it's just hype)
- **Real-World Case Studies**: Stories from teams who've transformed their marketing operations

## Who This Is For

If you're a CMO, VP of Marketing, or Marketing Ops leader at a B2B SaaS company running multiple campaigns across channels, this content is for you. We're especially focused on teams in the $20M–$200M ARR range who need enterprise-grade visibility without enterprise-grade complexity.
