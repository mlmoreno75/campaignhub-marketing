## What Is Campaign Governance?

Campaign governance is the framework of policies, processes, and tools that marketing teams use to plan, approve, execute, and measure their campaigns consistently. It ensures accountability, visibility, and alignment across stakeholders—from the CMO to individual campaign managers.

## Why B2B Marketing Teams Need Governance

Without governance, marketing campaigns become chaotic. Teams launch campaigns without proper approvals, budgets balloon without oversight, and measuring ROI becomes nearly impossible.

### The Cost of No Governance

- **Wasted budget** on duplicate or conflicting campaigns
- **Missed deadlines** due to unclear approval chains
- **Attribution gaps** that make ROI invisible to leadership
- **Team burnout** from firefighting instead of strategic work

## Building a Campaign Governance Framework

A strong governance framework includes:

1. **Clear roles and responsibilities** — Who approves what?
2. **Standardized workflows** — How do campaigns move from idea to launch?
3. **Centralized visibility** — Where can stakeholders see campaign status?
4. **Defined metrics** — What does success look like for each campaign type?
